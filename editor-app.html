How SEO Editor Works

What is SEO Editor?

SEO Editor is an tool which helps you write meaningful articles and blogs based on what your readers are searching on Google.


What SEO Editor can't do?

SEO Editor doesn't promise your articles will automatically rank higher. Instead, SEO Editor gives you a glimpse into what your readers are interested in reading.

How Google Search works

Ranking on Google Search depends on 100s of variables. But to boil it down, google's algorithm is always optimised, to make sure people find what they are looking for.

For users, a searcher on google, this is how google work:

  1. They search for what they want,
  2. scroll down and browse through titles,
  3. modify search query, when no title answer their question,
  4. open page with a title which promises an answer to their question,
  5. if page content doesn't answer their question, they come back to google search page,
  6. if page content answers their question, they read through the page.

For you, a website owner, this is how you experience it. Your ranking on search result rises up when:

  1. enough people search for your keyword,
  2. clicked on your link, and
  3. stayed on your page for a considerable duration.

For Google, the search engine, this means your page had enough value for these visitor, so maybe other people searching for the same keyword, will also find value in your page. When this happens enough times, your page ranking rises up.
